## üé® How to Use Alan

After installation, you can launch Alan by following these steps:

1. Open your Applications folder.
2. Find and double-click on the Alan icon.
3. Once the app opens, click anywhere in the active window to draw a border around it.

You can adjust the thickness and color of the border right from the settings menu in the app. More features include:

- Customizable border styles (dashed, solid, dotted).
- Option to save your settings for future use.
  
This makes it easy to remember your preferred settings every time you use the application.

## üí° Tips for Using Alan

- If you close Alan, the borders remain until you close the apps or unselect the border option in the menu.
